History

The Frothy Fingers Coffee Shop was founded by Jessa Hager in 2026. What began as a small dream soon grew into a cozy haven for coffee lovers. Frothy Fingers was built on the idea of creating a place where every cup brings joy, comfort, and warmth. With its signature foamy blends, peaceful ambiance, and heartfelt service, the shop quickly became a local favorite. Every corner of Frothy Fingers reflects passion, creativity, and dedication—making it more than just a coffee shop, but a space where stories are shared, memories are made, and good moods are brewed daily.

Feature of the Owner's Life'

The owners of Frothy Fingers Coffee are a hardworking husband and wife team. The husband, who is an engineer, is 35 years old, while the wife, Jessa Hager, who works in the city, is 33 years old. Jessa Hager is originally from Poblacion, Boljoon, Philippines. Even though both of them have full-time jobs, they dreamed of building a small business that could give their family an extra source of income. The one who attends to and watches over their business is their staff.

Success Story of the Business

Frothy Fingers Coffee began as a simple dream of a hardworking couple who wanted to build a small source of income for their family, even while balancing their full-time jobs. Through determination, patience, and the support of their staff who manage the shop daily, the owners steadily improved their business and built a welcoming place loved by the community of Poblacion, Boljoon. Their journey shows that with passion and teamwork, even small beginnings can grow into a meaningful and successful business.

Challenges Met by the Founder/Owner

The founders of Frothy Fingers Coffee faced several challenges as they started their business, including balancing their full-time jobs while managing the needs of the coffee shop. They struggled with limited time, financial constraints, and the pressure of learning how to run a business from scratch. There were also moments when customer flow was low, making it difficult to maintain daily operations. Despite these obstacles, the owners stayed committed, relied on their staff for support, and continued improving the shop until it became stable and trusted in the community.

How the owner's see the Business after 10years

Ten years from now, Frothy Fingers Coffee is seen as a well-established and trusted coffee shop in the community. The business is expected to grow with more loyal customers, improved services, and possibly additional branches. It will continue to provide quality coffee, stable income for the owners, and employment opportunities, while remaining a welcoming place for people to relax and connect.

How the Philippine Government Helped the Business Grow

The Philippine government helped businesses like Frothy Fingers Coffee grow by providing support programs for small businesses. This includes financial assistance through loans, training programs for business management, and guidance on health and safety regulations. These programs help owners learn how to improve their business, reach more customers, and make their shop safe and successful.
